Project: EZTABLE Red Envelope


I designed the innovative user referral program of EZTABLE, which successfully acquired 1% of the online population per month. It's also a social product for diners to engage with family and friends, aligning with the monetary social gift culture of red envelopes in East and Southeast Asia.

EZTABLE is the Asia-leading table reservation service, funded and coached by Dr. Clayton Christensen who coined Disruptive Innovation theory.

Project: $plitting

I designed "$plitting", an app for groups to constantly split multiple bills and keep a running total of who owes who what. Users can jot down expenses and split them within a click, and it will calculate and simplize mutual debts automatically. Make the tasks hassle free.

Designed for roommates to split household bills, travel companions to figure out trip costs, and friends who often spot cash for each other, to make sure that everyone gets paid back.
